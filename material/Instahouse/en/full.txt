Mod adds the ability to instantly create a 20 variety houses in your world. You will be able to build a small house in the woods, giant mansions and huge houses that looks better than a real castle!

Mod INSTANT HOUSE will be useful to all players, especially those who like to travel through the game world and far away from his home in MCPE. Now, if you are caught by surprise in the night, and you have collected a lot of material and are afraid of losing them, then you can very quickly build a refuge. This would be useful if you want to build a village, but did not want to spend a lot of time.

How it works? 
Go to the inventory of the creative mode and take one of the items (it looks like a house). Now just tap in any place and after a few seconds you will see the necessary house.
